PROVIDING SENIOR CHECK-IN CALLS - CARETOCALL

Peace of mind for seniors and their families who need to stay connected.

CareToCall is a pro-active, virtual assisted living service that helps seniors remain independent through daily senior care calls.

Even the most independent seniors can use a private network of family and friends to back them up. Pre-arranged, daily senior care calls are a simple, non-intrusive way to create a safety net for you and your loved ones.

No specialized technology or hardware is required. You simply register through the CareToCall website, specify which family and friends will be in your own private network, then decide when and how often you would like a senior check in phone call (prerecorded by you, a family member, or a friend). If you don’t answer, or your call goes to voicemail, your recorded message is forwarded to the trusted person at the top of your call list. Now they can check up on you to make sure everything is okay.

Medication reminders, live voice messages, calendar reminders, reassurance phonecalls and access for your most important family and friends are some of the helpful features you get with a monthly CareToCall subscription.

As a senior call service, CareToCall is proactive and is not only activated when your health or safety is being threatened.

While emergency call necklaces provide a reactive form of personal security, they are limited in what they can do. CareToCall takes a much broader approach to your personal well-being, before you are in crisis mode.

Family and friends have the reassurance, you are regularly keeping connected by receiving your personally recorded message, reminding you to take your medication, leave for an appointment, pay a bill, or any number of other predetermined events.
